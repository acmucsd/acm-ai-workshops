# 2. Getting Started

## 2.1 AI School 1 - Intro to Computer Vision, Python, and Data Preprocessing

{/*
    You can write something up for each workshop or use their marketing descriptions.
*/}

We'll kick things off with a brief introduction to AI and Python before diving into the fascinating world of computer vision. You will also hands-on experience working on a data preprocessing project!


## 2.2 AI School 2 - Deep Learning Foundations and Neural Network with PyTorch

{/*
    You can write something up for each workshop or use their marketing descriptions.
*/}

We’ll dive into some of the essential AI concepts—gradient descent and backpropagation—alongside a quick intro to PyTorch. And to top it off, you'll get hands-on experience with an image classification project!

## 2.3 AI School 3 - CNN and Neural Style Transfer

We’ll learn about deep learning — convolutional layers & receptive field — alongside transfer learning. And to top it off, you’ll get hands-on experience with an neural style transfer project! 

## 2.4 AI School 4 - Real-time Computer Vision

We'll be diving into real-time AI application deployment! This is your chance to see computer vision models in action and get hands-on experience with deployment, and share your work with others!
